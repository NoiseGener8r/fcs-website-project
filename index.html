We are Probably Bigfoot, an alt rock band hailing from Boston, Massachusetts

Popularly known for our unique musical style and inventive, comedic lyrics, our music conveys a range of emotions from roaring laughter to silent weeping. Out most famous album, Probably Bigfoot II: Electric Boogaloo, is a 10 part song epic detailing the adventures of a man attempting to revive disco in America.
